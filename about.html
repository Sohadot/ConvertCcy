Why Mid-Market Rate?

The mid-market rate — also called the interbank rate — is the midpoint between the buying and selling price of a currency. It's the rate you see on Google Finance and Reuters. Banks and exchange services add their own margins on top of this rate. By showing you the mid-market rate, ConvertCCY helps you understand the true value of your currency so you can spot fair deals and avoid overpaying.
