Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

🔒 Short version: ConvertCCY does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. We have no user accounts and no login system.

1. Information We Collect

ConvertCCY is a static website. We do not require registration or login. We do not collect your name, email, address, or any personally identifiable information during normal use of the currency converter.

When you use the contact form, you voluntarily provide your name and email address solely for the purpose of receiving a reply. This information is not stored in any database and is not shared with third parties.

2. Cookies and Tracking

ConvertCCY uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing activity. This is managed entirely by Google's advertising platform. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

We may also use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns (pages visited, time on site). This data is anonymized and does not identify individual users.

3. Exchange Rate Data

Exchange rates displayed on ConvertCCY are fetched in real-time from third-party financial data APIs. When your browser requests exchange rate data, your IP address may be temporarily logged by the API provider as part of standard server operations. ConvertCCY does not control or access these logs.

4. Third-Party Services

ConvertCCY uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies: Google Fonts (for typography), Google AdSense (for advertising), Exchange Rate API (for currency data), and Google Analytics (for traffic analysis). We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Security

ConvertCCY is served over HTTPS, ensuring all data transmitted between your browser and our server is encrypted. We do not store sensitive financial data or personal information on our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

ConvertCCY is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of ConvertCCY after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact page.
